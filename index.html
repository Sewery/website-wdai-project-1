What is it about?

The Great is a darkly funny, "historically inaccurate" romp through Catherine the Great’s rise to power. Trapped in marriage to Russia’s mad Emperor Peter, Catherine schemes, battles, and drinks her way toward the throne. It’s chaotic, hilarious, and wildly addictive—a twisted take on history you won’t forget!

Why should you watch it?

Watch The Great for a hilariously twisted take on history. With Elle Fanning as the ambitious Catherine and Nicholas Hoult as her unhinged husband, it’s sharp, funny, and addictive. Expect opulent costumes, outrageous plot twists, and laughs that turn Russian history into chaotic, must-watch fun. It's royally entertaining!
